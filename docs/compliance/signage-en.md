---

## Instructions

1. Fill in `[COMPANY NAME]` and `[EMAIL / PHONE]`
2. Print at A5 size or larger
3. Laminate for outdoor use
4. Post at **all entrances** to monitored areas
5. Ensure the sign is visible before entering the monitored zone
6. Remove or cover signs if camera surveillance is discontinued

## Accessibility

- The notice should also be available digitally (e.g., on the company intranet)
- For Swedish-speaking workers, also post the Swedish version (see `signage-sv.md`)
